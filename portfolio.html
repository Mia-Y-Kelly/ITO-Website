Portfolio

UP Portage Health

The UP Health team is revamping an application currently in use by UP Health System Marquette. The program is used by doctors, nurses, and physicians across the state to schedule 20,000+ annual telemedicine appointments. The team is working to bring the application up to security standards while simplifying the back-end software.

Porter Family Vineyard

In Fall 2017 IT Oxygen started working with Porter Family Vineyard on a business intelligence project. The vineyard is in Napa Valley, California and right in the heart of Silicon Valley. Students on this team helped to set up a database for the vineyard that would work with Power BI. In doing so, Power BI would then be able to showcase the vineyard's data in different visual formats.

Microsoft - Bing Growth

The Microsoft, Bing Growth Team is working to grow the usage of Microsoft search engine Bing by creating an extension for the Google Chrome browser. This extension is focusing on customizability and ease of use. The project also includes market analysis and creating ads to target specific markets.

Little Brothers Friends of the Elderly

The Little Brothers team is transitioning their client to a paramedic transportation scheduling system. The new system comes with key features like address management and driver availability. In Fall 2017, the Little Brothers team was invited to Ford Motor Company to present on the Little Brothers Project.
